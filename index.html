The Art of Hinting

Secret City Adventures Training

As Clue Staff, few elements of your role are as delicate and nuanced as hinting. Every team approaches puzzles differently, and what works for one group might fall flat with another. That's why it's an art, not a science.

By the end of this tutorial, you'll have learned to:

  • Deliver effective hints through questions rather than answers
  • Deploy (or restrain from using) certain tactics relative to the unique needs of players and teams
  • Manage pacing and difficulty while maintaining the immersive narrative
  • Balance challenge with enjoyment, knowing when to let players struggle and when to intervene
