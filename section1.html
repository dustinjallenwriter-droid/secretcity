The Golden Rule

"Do not hint unless absolutely necessary."

The ideal game is for the players to solve everything on their own without any hints from the clue-staff. Players do not want to be told the answers—they are paying to be challenged. Hinting should only be utilized to avoid a level of frustration that hinders the players' enjoyment or to avoid them missing the majority of the game due to lack of experience, low player numbers, or any other anomaly.
