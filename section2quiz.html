Correct

This scenario highlights the need to divide tasks among players. With everyone focused on one puzzle, others go unsolved and some players become passive spectators.

Which universal reminder fits this situation?

✓

Correct

When players aren't sharing discoveries, the team wastes time duplicating work. A gentle reminder to communicate keeps everyone on the same page.

Which universal reminder would help here?

✓

Correct

When players are rushing and missing clues, encouraging them to slow down and be thorough helps them catch what they've overlooked.
